Lessons Learned

  • Do not trust obvious strings immediately, especially in reverse challenges.
  • Document why a flag is fake, not only what the real flag is.
  • Translate assembly into pseudocode before writing a solver.
  • Keep every useful command, output and reasoning step for future review.
