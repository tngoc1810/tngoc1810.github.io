About

Blue team mindset, offensive curiosity.

My learning path combines SOC fundamentals with CTF practice so I can understand both attacker behavior and defender response.

I am a second-year Information Security student at the Academy of Cryptography Techniques (KMA). My main goal is to become a SOC Analyst and, in the long term, a Threat Hunter who can investigate real incidents, understand adversary behavior, and turn evidence into reliable detection logic.
