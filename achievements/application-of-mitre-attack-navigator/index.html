Achievement Summary Demonstrated ability to use ATT&CK Navigator to quickly visualize an organization’s security posture by mapping out techniques, tactics, and procedures (TTPs) used by attackers.

Overview

This guide provided an overview of ATT&CK Navigator, a tool developed by the MITRE Corporation to help organizations gain visibility into their security posture. The ATT&CK Navigator also allows users to compare their organization’s posture over time and assess defensive coverage against specific adversaries.
